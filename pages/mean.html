Making use of mean in real life

Scenario

In this example, let's imagine you are a waiter in a restaurant.
You are very curious of knowing how much tip do you get on average.
With that in mind, you went on to record 5 tips you've gotten.

tips (in US dollars) = [5, 17, 11, 8, 14, 5]

How to get the mean

  1. Add up all the values in the data set.
    5 + 17 + 11 + 8 + 14 + 5 = 60
  2. Divide it by the number of values.
    60 / 6 = 10
    mean tips = $10
